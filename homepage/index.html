I hate to say this and it's sad to see - but the state of the game now is awful. Yes it plays quite well and visually looks better than ever - but it's really lost that PUBG magic and feel. Empty feeling lobbies with bots filling numbers, a new menu / system every time I log on it feels like, and so many micro transactions. Everything just feels cluttered and complicated as soon as you log on with a very messy UI. When you get into a lobby you're now greeted by people running around as multi coloured anime looking characters/skins, and I'm pretty sure Barney the dinosaur ran past me at one point... It's like it's suddenly lost it's own identity and copied Fortnite?! It really feels like the Devs completely lost the roadmap and made the rest up as they went along, stealing other games recipes and jumbling it up into one mess. I really hate to write this as PUBG was one of my favourite games of all time. Very let down.

I experienced a quiet terror in my first PlayerUnknown's BattleGrounds (PUBG) match. The second one elicited a similar feeling. The third match was much better than the previous ones, as I managed to carefully drive around on a motorbike until fewer than 10 players remained. The next time, I was slowly killed off for not staying within the rescinding play area boundaries. Such is life while playing PUBG. Despite some frustrating experiences, PUBG's great visuals, fun premise, and massive player base make it a solid PC and Xbox One title.

As you can see, I have an unreasonable amount of time spent on this game. After playing pubg for a solid two years after its release in 2017 I can safely say that this game is not worth anyone's time anymore. The "pay to look good" cosmetic reworks only really benefit people who want to spend money on a game that loses more and more of its player base everyday. "Let me buy all these skins so the BOTS I kill can see how cool i look". The straw that broke the camel's back for me was my group's sudden return to this game about 2 weeks ago to "test the waters again". After 2 weeks of play one of my close friends RECEIVED A 3 DAY BAN as he was about to eliminate the last man in our game and win a chicken dinner. I've played this game for 4 years and I or any of my friends have never received a ban for any reason whatsoever. Apparently if one is good at the game, and gets reported enough times, they receive a ban WHILE THEY ARE INVESTIGATED. As a business that is slowly losing its customer base, the worst possible thing you could do is ban a player temporarily ONLY based on how many people have reported them. What you should be doing is investigating them while they play or if not closely after they finish games so you actually have solid evidence before you ban someone. Krafton can spout back rules, regulations, guidelines, and policies all they want. But coming back to a game and getting banned for not doing anything wrong, makes my friends and I feel like uninstalling the game and never playing it again. Bye pubg its been fun I hope the investigation goes well for you guys cause you lost 6 players, with this 3 day ban, forever. Deuces
